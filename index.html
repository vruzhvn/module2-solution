Our Menu

Besh

Beshbarmaq is a hearty dish made from boiled meat, including chicken, veal, or horse meat, served on large flat noodles. The meat is typically cooked with onions and sometimes served with a rich broth called shorpa, poured over the noodles.

Quyrdaq

Quyrdaq is a traditional dish made from fried meat and offal, such as liver, heart, and kidneys, usually from chicken, veal, or horse. The meat is often cooked with onions and seasoned with salt and pepper.

Bauyrsaq

Bauyrsaq are small, fried dough balls that resemble doughnuts but are typically less sweet. Made from a simple dough of flour, yeast, milk, and butter, they are deep-fried to a golden brown.
